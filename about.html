PRODUCT OVERVIEW

The BeanBrew coffee shop is a cozy and inviting space designed for people to relax, work, or socialize while enjoying high-quality coffee and light refreshments. It offers a variety of coffee beverages, including espresso, lattes, and cold brews. The atmosphere combines modern aesthetics with warm, rustic charm, featuring comfortable seating, ambient lighting, and free Wi-Fi. Friendly baristas provide excellent customer service, creating a welcoming environment for both regulars and new visitors. Whether you're grabbing a quick coffee to-go or spending time reading or chatting with friends, the coffee shop caters to a wide range of customer needs.

OUR STORY

One cool evening in Bacolod, two friends, Javi and Miguel, sat on their apartment balcony, gazing at the city lights. "I wish we could get good coffee at this hour,". Most cafés were closed, and convenience store coffee just didn’t hit the same. "What if we opened a place that served real coffee—even at night?". That simple wish became BeanBrew—a coffee shop where night owls, students, and creatives could enjoy specialty coffee long after others had closed. But for Javi and Miguel, it wasn’t just about late hours. They wanted coffee that told a story—one rooted in local farmers, sustainability, and bold flavors.

SUSTAINABLE SOURCE

By supporting M3CG, BeanBrew helps uplift marginalized farmers while keeping Negros’ coffee heritage alive. Negrense Native Organic Coffee – Kanlaon, Negros Occidental Grown on the volcanic slopes of Mt. Kanlaon, these heirloom Barako and Liberica beans are 100% organic. The rich soil gives the coffee a deep, earthy flavor with hints of chocolate and spice—perfect for cold brew. Why it matters: These farmers use zero synthetic inputs, protecting both the environment and the unique taste of native Negrense coffee

OUR MISSION

At BeanBrew Coffee Shop, our mission is to serve high-quality, sustainably sourced coffee while supporting local farmers and promoting eco-friendly practices. We are committed to creating a warm, welcoming space for our community, where every cup makes a positive impact on both people and the planet.

OUR VISION

Our vision is to become a leading example of a community-focused, environmentally conscious coffee shop that inspires others to value sustainability, support local agriculture, and enjoy coffee with purpose.
